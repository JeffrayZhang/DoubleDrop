Objective

The objective of Tetris is to score as many points as possible by clearing horizontal lines of blocks.

Controls

Use the following keyboard controls to play the game:


Player 1

  • A: Move the tetromino left
  • D: Move the tetromino right
  • S: Move the tetromino down
  • Q: Rotate the tetromino left
  • E: Rotate the tetromino right

Player 2

  • Left Arrow: Move the tetromino left
  • Right Arrow: Move the tetromino right
  • Down Arrow: Move the tetromino down
  • Up Arrow: Rotate the tetromino

Gameplay

The game starts with an empty grid. Tetrominoes, which are geometric shapes that will fall from the top of the grid. Use the arrow and wasd keys to move and rotate the falling tetrominoes to fit them together at the bottom.

When a horizontal line of blocks is completely filled, it will be cleared, and you will earn points. As you clear more lines, the game will get faster, increasing the difficulty.

Game Over

If the stack of blocks reaches the top of the grid, the game is over or the time is up. Your final score will be displayed, and you can choose to play again if you wish.
